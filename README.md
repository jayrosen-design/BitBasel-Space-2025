**New Worlds Reading AR Expeditions**, developed at the **University of Florida College of Education**, is an immersive educational initiative aimed at addressing literacy gaps among 3rdâ€“5th grade students. Designed to complement the **New Worlds Reading Initiative**, the project provides **free books shipped to homes**, and pairs them with **augmented reality (AR) mini-games** that reinforce vocabulary, reading confidence, and curiosity.

- Students scan floating AR bubbles using tablets  
- Prompts reveal life-scale models of **whales**, **dolphins**, **clownfish**, **octopuses**, and more  
- Gameplay reinforces vocabulary, comprehension, and ecological awareness
